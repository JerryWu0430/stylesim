## 1. Customer Segments

| Segment | Size | Pain Intensity | Willingness to Pay |
|---------|------|----------------|-------------------|
| **Mid-market brands** ($10M-500M revenue) | ~5,000 globally | High (35% unsold inventory avg) | $50-300K/yr |
| **Fast fashion companies** | ~200 major players | Very High (speed + waste pressure) | $100K-500K/yr |
| **Private label manufacturers** | ~2,000 | Medium-High (retail margin pressure) | $30-100K/yr |
| **Emerging DTC brands** | ~10,000+ | Medium (cash-constrained) | $20-60K/yr |

**Primary target:** Mid-market brands - large enough to have budget, small enough to lack in-house analytics

---

## 2. Value Propositions

| Value Type | Proposition | Quantified Impact |
|------------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Risk Reduction** | Test 100+ designs, produce top 20 | 40-60% reduction in failed SKUs |
| **Cost Reduction** | Minimize overstock/markdowns | 30%+ reduction in end-of-season discounts |
| **Performance** | AI predicts style-market fit | 25% higher sell-through rate |
| **Convenience** | Replace gut instinct with data | Weeks → hours for collection decisions |
| **Brand/Status** | Sustainability story | Reduce fashion waste, improve ESG scores |

**Core message:** "Know what sells before you make it"

---

## 3. Channels

| Phase | Channel | Rationale |
|-------|---------|-----------|
| **Awareness** | Trade shows (MAGIC, Première Vision), LinkedIn, fashion trade press | Industry is relationship-driven |
| **Evaluation** | Free pilot simulation, case studies, ROI calculator | Prove value with their own designs |
| **Purchase** | Direct sales (enterprise), self-serve (SMB) | High ACV needs consultative sale |
| **Delivery** | Cloud SaaS + API integrations (PLM, ERP) | Fit existing workflows |
| **After-sales** | Customer success, quarterly reviews, user community | Retention = expansion |

## Validation Questions

1. **Data moat** - can competitors license same trend data? Defensibility?
2. **Simulation accuracy** - what prediction accuracy threshold needed for adoption?
3. **Integration complexity** - how long to integrate with legacy PLM/ERP? Deal-breaker?
4. **Buyer** - who owns budget: design, merchandising, or finance?
5. **Proof points** - need beta customers before funding?
6. **Competitor response** - what if EDITED/Trendalytics builds this?
7. **Fast fashion fit** - do they move too fast for simulation cycles?
8. **Data cold start** - how to train models without customer data?
